HOW IT WORKS

Add hotspots to your image

Want to highlight an area, object, person or context in your image? Just add a hotspot to it with a label.Adding them while zooming in makes them “to the point”.

Enrich your hotspots. Make them interesting.

Add text or audio recoridng to each of your hotspots. Not enough? No Problem. Add media to them like URL, Images, Video or documents. Make them shine.
