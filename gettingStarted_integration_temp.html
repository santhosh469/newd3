Adding Hotspots to your Images

You can pick an image from gallery or click one using your camera without levaing the app. You can find create options from the Feed screen, the Menu or from your Vault as well.

Once an image is picked it loads inside the Hotspot Mode. You can add a Hotspot from the main Menu at the bottom. By default the Hotspot lands on the center of your screen on top of your image. Drag the hotspot and place it wherever you want on the image.

By default the hotspot menu is opened below when you add the hotspot or when you touch it again. You can add a #labelto the hotspot, change its color and add details to it.
These attributes can be changed, deleted and edited at any time. Click anywhere outside and get the main menu at the bottom.

RNYOO TIP:
You can zoom in on the image and add a hotspot and the zoomed position would be retained on the hotspot.

Add Data and Attachments to your Hotspots

You can click on add details on the hotspot menu at the bottom and continue adding data and attachments to your hotspot.

- You can add a description for your hotspot( upto 150 characters) and can also add an URL to it
- You can record an audio for 30 seconds for your hotspot
- You can further enhance your hotspot by adding media to it. Click on Add Media to get your Media options
- You can add an URL to your hotspot
- You can add images to your hotspot (upto 4 images and you can click an image or pick from gallery)
- You can add a video to your hotspot. (Record a 30 seconds video or pick a video from your gallery)
- You can add a document (upto 2MB and PDF, DOC and DOCX is allowed)

RNYOO TIP:
When you pick a video from gallery it loads into a custom clipper and you can clip a 30 secs duration to upload

Sharing Hotspots - Selectively Share your Hotspots

One of the most amazing features of Rnyoo is Selective Sharing of Hotspots.
When you proceed to Posting your content Rnyoo gives you the option of assigning your hotspots to your individual Friends and Groups. You can select a hotspot from top and select from your Friends and Groups at the bottom. When done assigning the hotspots just go ahead to the next step.

Your assignees wil receive the post on their Friends or Groups Feed based on the assignment but when they dive into it they can only see the hotspots assigned to them. Isn’t this amazing? You can also use Multiselect to select multiple hotspots and assign them together. Use Lock to save your assignments.

Incase you want to create a Public Post skip the selective sharing part, as all Public Posts content are for everyone on Rnyoo. Show off your content to the world.

RNYOO TIP:
- Incase you want to just share the image and no hotspots with someone on your network, select the image icon and assign it to them. In this case they just see the image and no hotspots.
- You can also keep any of your hotspots private and not share it with anyone. In this case only you can view it.
- You can always change assignments and update your post and the Post and Hotspots would be available for the changed assignees.
- You can also add new hotspots on your POST and assign them fresh.

How to interact with Posts?

On Rnyoo you can create Posts on your Friends and Groups Feed or on Public. You can also interact with Posts that are shared with you on your Timeline or on Groups with you or on the Public Feed. Interaction with Posts are on multiple levels.

You can view posts on the different Timelines - Public, Following, Friends and Groups. You can scroll through them. Want to delve into it? Open the Summary View. The Summary View gives you overall information and stats on the Post. You can also comment on the Post from here. You can Share the Post(if its a Public Post) via Social, Web, Email or on Rnyoo Messaging. You can also set a Reminder for the Post for a time and date to remind you to view it. You can also view the Post Level Media if shared by the Publisher. Like the Post if you find it interesting.

Want to view and interact with the hotspots, dive deeper into the Drilldown View. Tap on the hotspots and view its information on the bottom. Want to converse on the hotspots with the Publisher? SImple. Just start a commenting thread on the hotspot.

See something interesting on the picture and want to know more abou? Place a question like a hotspot and ask the Publisher about it.

RNYOO TIP:
- Want to stay tuned with a Public Post? Hit Follow this Post button and it will get added to your Follwing Feed. Now you will receive notifications for it whenver something changes on it.

How to use your Vault?

The Rnyoo Vault is your personal storage of PODs(Images with Hotspots). Save your work of adding hotspots and information in your Vault and get back to it again from your Vault. Maybe you need more time for it? Or curate your own personal content. Store them all in your Vault.

You can tag your Vault Images with common tags to group them together. Search by a Tag Name to filter your PODs on Vault.

Sync your Vault between multiple devices to get latest PODs. We also store your Vault PODs on the cloud so you can also safely delete them from your device and still get access to them whenever you need them again.

RNYOO TIP:
- All Vault PODs are in unpublished state. Once you POST them there are removed from your Vault.
- When your POST is deleted or expired it goes back to your Rnyoo Cloud Vault and you can again Sync them back to your device when you need it again.

How to create your Network?

On Rnyoo you can find and add users and create your network. You can search for users using their Rnyoo Nickname(which is unique for all users) or their user names. You can send Friend Requests to users and when they accept you are connected.

You can also create Groups on Rnyoo. Select a few of your friends on Rnyoo and create a Group. Maybe create a family group, hobbies group or anything that you want to. Groups gives you the ability to share content directy on them. Your friends can also add you to their groups.

Still not finding anyone on Rnyoo from your known circles? Simply Invite them over. Once they join you can add them.
